Yichi Zhang

Computer Science & Economics • Mechanism Design • Information Elicitation • Human–AI Collaboration

I am a researcher working at the intersection of computer science and economics. My work focuses on information elicitation and aggregation, peer-prediction mechanisms, and human–AI collaboration to incentivize high‑effort, honest human feedback and to robustly use imperfect data.
